5. For u+sx:
u refers to the user's permissions, + refers to adding a permission, and sx are the permission arguments.
s refers to special permission, and x refers to execute permission
so u+sx will change the permissions so that the user will be able to execute this file with special (root) permissions.

For o-w,
o refers to other users that aren't the user or the group, - refers to removing a permission, and w is the permission argument.
So o-w will remove other users' write permission for the file.

Combined, u+sx and o-w will change their respective permissions when combined with a comma.
